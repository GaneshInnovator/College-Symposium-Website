FIRE BALL

VV COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

TISAIYANVILAI

venue:Conference hall

Wed, Oct 04, 9:00 AM EDT

This game includes perfect offence and defense. Two players per team total 2 teams allowed for a round.A table will be allocated for the two teams in opposite direction. Several plastic cups will be given for each team and one member of each team is asked to arrange the cups in a pyramid order. The other member will be provided with sponge ball by which the member can attack the opponents cups. Players can attack with the ball and also defend the attack from ball. Between certain time limit the team which has ordered maximum cups will be considered as winners.
